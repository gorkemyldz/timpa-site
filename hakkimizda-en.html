Who Are We?

With our production approach that does not compromise on hygiene and quality, we are opening up to the world in the disposable textile sector.

TİMPA LTD. CO.

As TİMPA, we produce critical hygiene products such as disposable towels, neck strips, and couch covers with 100% local capital in our modern production facility in Pendik, Istanbul.

With a monthly production capacity of 10 million pieces and exports to more than 15 countries, we continue to be a reliable partner not only in Turkey but also in the global market. We offer professional solutions to hairdressers, beauty salons, and health centers with touch-free production in ISO 9001 standards.

Our Mission

To raise hygiene standards in the sector by delivering our high-quality sterile products from the manufacturer to the consumer at the most affordable cost.

Our Vision

To become the leading manufacturer in the European and Middle Eastern markets for disposable hygiene products with our innovative production technologies.
